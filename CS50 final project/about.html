What is Conversation Starter?

This is a webpage where you can generate fun questions. It can be especially useful for language learners and teachers to practice conversation in a foreign language. And it is ideal for those who dread small talks because they never know what to ask. Your solution is right here.

Every question is available in 4 languages: English, Dutch, Hungarian and Italian. These are the languages I speak or learn.
